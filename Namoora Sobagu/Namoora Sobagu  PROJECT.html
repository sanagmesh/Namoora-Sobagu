About Us

Classic South Indian Namoora Sobagu Restaurant

Experience the authentic taste of Bangalore.

At Namoora Sobagu, we celebrate the city's most famous dishes, offering an authentic culinary journey through Bangalore's rich food heritage. Using traditional recipes and high-quality ingredients, we ensure each dish captures the essence of local flavors. Founded by food lovers passionate about Bangalore's cuisine, Namoora Sobagu quickly became a go-to guide for exploring the city's iconic foods.
